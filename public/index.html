You must be signed into the same Meta account on both devices.

Tips for best results

  • Use a 5 GHz Wi-Fi network — same network on both devices.
  • Keep your Chromebook plugged in to avoid throttling.
  • Use Google Chrome (not another browser) for the receiver tab.
  • If the stream lags, lower your Quest's graphics settings under Settings → Graphics Preferences.
  • For a fullscreen view, press F4 or use Chrome's fullscreen button.
